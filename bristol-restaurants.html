Restaurants and cafes

Discover the best vegan and plant-based dining experiences in Bristol

Plantuguese

The UK's ONLY 100% Vegan Portuguese Restaurant. Experience authentic plant-based Portuguese cuisine in the heart of Bristol. Located at 34 Stokescroft, BS1 3QD, serving the best Portuguese inspired foods for vegans and non-vegans to enjoy. Open Monday-Sunday 12pm-10pm.

100% Vegan Authentic Portuguese

VX Bristol

The first 100% vegan boutique in the UK, now in Bristol! Offering a full junk food menu featuring kebabs, burgers, cheeseburgers, milkshakes, cakes, and French patisserie. Also a vegan grocery store with imported vegan goods, cheeses, mock-meat, and hard-to-find products. Located at 123 East Street, BS3 4ER. Open Tuesday-Friday 9am-4pm, Saturday 9am-4pm, Sunday 11am-4pm. Kitchen closes 30 minutes before closing time.

100% Vegan Junk Food Vegan Grocery
Visit Restaurant

Oowee Vegan

The birth place of Oowee Vegan! An easy-going stop for plant-based vegan fast food. Located at 65 Baldwin Street, BS1 1QZ, serving deliciously vegan comfort food including classic double cheeseburgers, jalapeño cheese fries, signature milkshakes, and more. Offering delivery, click & collect, and dine-in options. Walk-in only, no bookings. Open Monday-Wednesday 11:30am-10pm, Thursday 11:30am-10:30pm, Friday-Saturday 11:30am-11pm, Sunday 11:30am-10:30pm.

100% Vegan Fast Food
Visit Restaurant

Cheung's Vegan Kitchen

Family-run Chinese takeaway established in 1969, now offering an all-vegan menu with a modern twist on traditional Chinese cuisine. Located at 15 Sandhurst Road, Brislington, BS4 3PJ. Serving dishes including Crispy Aromatic Vegan Duck, Sweet and Sour Vegan Chicken Balls, Premium Vegan Beef with Black Bean Sauce, Malaysian Vegan Curry, and Vegan Chicken Satay Skewers. Using fresh ingredients from local farms and mock meats from trusted suppliers. Open Tuesday-Saturday 5pm-9:30pm. Closed Monday and Sunday.

100% Vegan Chinese Cuisine Family-Run
Visit Restaurant

Fi Real Caribbean Vegan

Vegan Caribbean Restaurant located in Bristol sharing the experience of Caribbean food in a unique and tasty way. Experience vegan cuisine like you've never tasted before. Located at 57 West Street, Old Market, BS2 0BZ. Open Sunday 4pm-8pm, Monday-Thursday 4pm-9:30pm. Closed Friday and Saturday. Open on some bank holidays - please ring to confirm. Booking recommended.

100% Vegan Caribbean Cuisine
Visit Restaurant

East Village Cafe

Plant-based cafe in the heart of Clifton Village, Bristol. Serving a lovely selection of sandwiches, pastries, homemade cakes, seasonal drinks, iced coffees, smoothies, natural rainbow lattes, teas, and great coffee from Triple Co Roast from Stokes Croft in Bristol. Well-behaved dog friendly! Located at Arch House, Boyces Avenue, Clifton Village, BS8 4AA. Joined by sister company French Grey, selling beautiful jewellery, wall art, gifts and homewares.

Plant-Based Cafe Dog Friendly
Visit Cafe

Vegan India

Bristol's first 100% Vegan Indian restaurant and Bristol's favourite vegan Indian food! Located on Gloucester Road at 189 Gloucester Road, BS7 8BG. Proudly serving plant-based menu with authentic Indian dishes including plant-based chicken korma, meatless kofta chilli masala, meatless meatballs, and delicious vegetables with exotic Indian spices. Restaurant capacity of 60 seats. Special offers: 20% off on collection orders and £15 Banquet Deal (Sunday to Thursday).

100% Vegan Indian Cuisine Bristol's First

Koocha Mezze Bar

Bristol's first fully vegan restaurant, established in 2018! Experience plant-powered Persian and Middle Eastern cuisine as you never have before. Located at 203B Cheltenham Road, BS6 5QX. Showcasing the best in Persian and Middle Eastern cooking with fresh, colourful and creative plant-based dishes. Serving mezze dishes, vegan doner kebabs, signature cocktails and mocktails inspired by the Middle East. Committed to sustainability with locally sourced ingredients. Open Monday-Thursday 12pm-11pm, Friday-Saturday 12pm-11:30pm, Sunday 12pm-10pm. Limited Sunday roasts available.

100% Vegan Persian & Middle Eastern Bristol's First Vegan
