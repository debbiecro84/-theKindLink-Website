Discover the Best Plant-Based Companion Animal Food & Product Brands in the UK

Finding truly vegan and ethical companion animal care products can be challenging. Many companion animal food brands rely on animal agriculture, and companion animal accessories may contain animal-derived materials like leather or wool. This page brings together everything you need – from nutritionally complete plant-based companion animal food to cruelty-free toys and accessories – all designed to keep your companion animals healthy while aligning with vegan values.

Below you'll find our companion animal care categories, each featuring carefully curated vegan brands. This directory will continue to grow as new ethical companion animal care brands are discovered, so feel free to bookmark and check back.

UK-based vegan pet food brand creating nutritionally complete plant-based food for dogs and cats since 2005. Their range includes vegan dog food, puppy food, cat food, treats, and compostable poop bags. All products are formulated to meet nutritional requirements while being completely free from animal ingredients. Committed to feeding change, one bowl at a time, with products that save countless lives and protect the planet.
