Plates Restaurant London

Fine dining plant-based restaurant by chef Kirk Haworth, a leading figure in British plant-based cookery. Located at 320 Old Street, featuring seasonal menus of nature-inspired creativity with a unique style of fruit and vegetable cookery. Open Wednesday-Saturday with lunch and dinner service, offering intimate dining for up to 6 people per table.

100% Plant-Based Fine Dining

Gauthier Soho

Elegant fine dining restaurant in the heart of Soho, London. Located at 21 Romilly Street, offering sophisticated cuisine with lunch service on Saturdays and dinner Tuesday-Saturday. Features private dining options, caviar service, and voucher system. Open for lunch Saturday 1pm, dinner Tuesday-Thursday 5-10pm, Friday-Saturday 5-10:30pm.

Studio Gauthier

Alexis Gauthier's exciting new restaurant featuring a 100% animal-free menu in a relaxed, chic setting. Located at BFI Stephen Street alongside 123V Bakery, offering refined modern seasonal cuisine with global influences. Features bottomless sushi, self-serve buffet, cocktails, and group-friendly booths. Open Tuesday-Saturday 12pm-11pm, fully wheelchair accessible.

100% Plant-Based Group Friendly

Mali Vegan Thai

Authentic Thai vegan restaurant born from family recipes, bringing traditional Thai plant-based cuisine to London. Located at 5a Hogarth Place, offering simple everyday dishes from Thai culture where veganism has a 200+ year tradition. Family-run establishment serving lunch and dinner with dine-in and takeaway options.

100% Vegan Family-Run

Club Mexicana

Plant-based flavor ravers serving tacos, nachos, and burritos like you've never seen before! Multiple central London locations including Soho, Boxhall City, and Mercato Mayfair. Famous for frozen margaritas and the absolute best vibes. Dog-friendly, wheelchair accessible, and offering takeaway, delivery, and dine-in options. Open daily from 12noon.

100% Plant-Based Mexican Cuisine

Family-owned Caribbean restaurant in Angel, Islington founded in 2020 by siblings Jordan and Chyna. Featured in SquareMeal Top 100 London Restaurants and acclaimed by The Times, The Guardian, and British Vogue for revolutionary plant-based cuisine.

Plant-Based Caribbean Award-Winning
