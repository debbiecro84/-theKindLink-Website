Luxury vegan glamping in the Cambridgeshire countryside. Stay in luxury safari tents with wood-fired hot tubs, complete kitchen facilities, and luxury shower rooms. Perfect for families and groups up to 6, with beautiful fenland views and access to woodland walks.

The Base Vegan Retreat Animal Sanctuary

Luxury vegan retreat and animal sanctuary in Bristol. Stay in a bespoke cabin surrounded by rescued animals with views over the Cotswolds. Features outdoor spa area, cooked vegan breakfast, and supports their not-for-profit animal sanctuary. A truly unique experience combining luxury accommodation with animal advocacy.

Bristol Animal Sanctuary 100% Vegan

La Crisalida Retreats

Holistic health and wellness retreat in the Upper Severn Valley, Wales. Stay in the historic Maesmawr Hall with daily yoga, meditation, guided walks, and plant-based nutrition. All-inclusive retreats with flexible arrival dates and optional holistic treatments. Perfect for reconnecting, rebalancing and re-energising.

Upper Severn Valley, Wales Plant-Based Nutrition Holistic Wellness

Haye Cornwall

Unique holidays and wellness retreats at an animal sanctuary in Cornwall. Stay in beautiful countryside accommodation surrounded by rescued donkeys, ponies, chickens, and cats. Enjoy yoga retreats, wild swimming walks, and vegan day retreats while supporting their animal rescue work and permaculture garden.

Cornwall Animal Sanctuary Permaculture Garden

Nurture yourself in nature at this serene Cornish garden spa retreat. Grown over thirty years from seed, this sanctuary offers peace, harmony and a step closer to mother nature. Experience glamping accommodation, holistic treatments, and wellness activities in a beautiful natural setting.
